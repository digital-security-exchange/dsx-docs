    2018-2019 Workplan¶

    Objective 1: Ensure a self-sustaining, independent, and responsive DSX¶

    Activities & Outputs¶

    1. Develop governance and organizational structure of independent DSX
    2. January and once a month thereafter - Hold team transition and strategy meeting
    3. Feb 2019- Work with stakeholders to adapt governance model and documentation for new model
    4. Feb 2019 - Coherent and transparent offerings of DSX.
    5. March 2018 and quarterly thereafter - Hold quarterly Advisory Committee meetings
    6. March 2019 - Financial support model and documentation for new members
    7. March 2019 - Share out these baseline services, including security policy development and training
    8. April 2019 - Hire Managing Director, Technology Director and Case Management Director
    9. June 2019 - Spin out of DSX
    10. April 2019 - Hold team transition and strategy meeting (IFF)
    11. April 2019 - Hold CDR/DSX strategy meeting (IFF)
    12. Nov 2019 - Prepare DSX five year business plan
    13. Work to ensure community health and trust in DSX
    14. March 2019 - Create an online community for members to receive updates and share information with each other
    15. Every other month -Encourage community trust building exercises (onsite visits, virtual calls and seminars on topics- top alerts, trends, new technologies, newsletter)
    16. Annually - Participation in community in-person events - RightsCon, Open Technology Fund Summit, Internet Freedom Festival
    17. Jan 2019 and quarterly thereafter - Solicit and implement feedback from stakeholder communities
    18. Ensure administrative and technical support is provided for
    19. Dec 2018 - Transition to Zammad platform
    20. February 2019 and quarterly thereafter - Share feedback to CDR about DSX’s cases, goals, process pain points, assistance needed, etc.
    21. April 2019 - Hire Director, Technology Director and Case Management Director
    22. April 2019 - Develop and document Cyber Squad utilization process and procedures
    23. June 2019 - Develop and document organisational policies
    24. Every other month - Intentional Cyber Squad outreach and growth
    25. Transition to usage of CDR Link platform
    26. Dec 2018 - Transition to Zammad Link platform
    27. Feb 2019 - Integrate Link platform with frontend websites and forms
    28. Feb 2019 - Technical case tracking process formalized and documented
    29. May 2019 - Connect network of global providers from CDR to DSX Link platform
    30. Nov 2019 - Zammad training manual prepared (CDR)
    31. Outreach to funders and partners to ensure sustainable and responsive funding
    32. Feb 2019 - Communicate new plan to existing funder contacts
    33. Feb 2019 - Develop fundraising strategy for 2019, including support for new staff
    34. March 2019 - Develop and formalize service contract structures and fees for services

    Objective 2: Improve collective understanding of the risks faced by the U.S. members, their community and how to mitigate them¶

    Activities & Outputs¶

    1. Onboard more U.S. members and expand activities to subgrantees
    2. March 2019 and monthly afterward - Outreach to potential new members
    3. March 2019 and monthly/as needed thereafter - Onboarding of new members
    4. Dec 2019 - Grow members by 10-12
    5. Produce and share community threat notifications and alerts
    6. March 2019 and monthly thereafter - Produce and share monthly threat notifications
    7. March 2019 and monthly/as needed thereafter - Produce and share community alerts in English in response to urgent community threats
    8. March 2019 - From CDR, hire Security Manager to work on researching and writing notifications and advising on mitigation measures
    9. May 2019 and monthly thereafter - Share non-sensitive information from threat notifications with key partners for wider community distribution
    10. Facilitate preventative interventions
    11. January 2019 and monthly/as needed thereafter - Facilitate assessments, training and security audits to partners
    12. March 2019 and monthly thereafter - Provide security advice to partners
    13. December 2019 - Grow our U.S. based service providers by 10
    14. December 2019 - From CDR, Develop advice handbook resource based on incident responses and share with global rapid responders’ network

    Objective 3: Optimize ‘incident response’ available to member communities¶

    Activities & Outputs¶

    1. Facilitate effective and efficient response to members and digital security incidents
    2. January 2019 and monthly thereafter - Facilitate incident reporting via CDR Link platform
    3. January 2019 and monthly thereafter - Provide security advice and consultation, bringing all members up to a baseline with ongoing support available
    4. Help shape the operating environment
    5. March 2019 and monthly/as needed thereafter - Create, document and implement a communication strategy for those who’ve been helped by DSX to stay connected and request assistance through Link
    6. May 2019 and monthly thereafter - Share trend analysis reports with wider community (blogs, tweets, etc.)
    7. As needed only - Convene activities with wider community as needed (community meetings, events, etc.)
